About Me

My favorite thing in life is to build something from scratch;
to be able to contribute to something that goes from nothing to beautiful.
Sometimes it's a website, or a piece of software, and sometimes it's a human life.
I have spent my professional career doing both, as a developer and a recovery professional.
Being in recovery myself I always enjoy watching people people's lives change for the better,
and I enjoy helping them along that journey even more. Working with computers is my true passion,
and the most clear expression of my inner self that I have yet found.
It truly has come naturally to me, and I've made it a point to never stop learning.

I have a passion for education, and seriously can't get enough of it.
A lot of my free time is dedicated to learning new things about a plethora of subjects,
and it has become a never ending journey for me.
